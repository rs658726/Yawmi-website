Daily Planning.

Yawmi is a productivity application that allows you to manage your tasks, view your calendar, and integrate into your workflow various third party tools such as Gmail or Github.

Download on the App Store

Features

Offline-First

Manage your tasks offline. Your data is automatically sync'd once you are back online.

Loginless

Seemlessly authenticate with your iCloud account. No need for a login or a password.

Private

Your data is stored in your iCloud private database and is end-to-end encrypted.

Integrative

Leverage the power of integrations to increase your productivity.

Intertwined workflows for an overall better productivity.

Integrations

Apple Calendar

Enable Apple Calendar access and consult your daily events directly in Yawmi. Toggle individual calendars active and conveniently join meetings at the click of a button thanks to Yawmi's detection algorithm.

Gmail

Connect your Gmail account to Yawmi and turn any email into a task, add a reminder, a due date, or some notes. The Yawmi Gmail integration also allows you to view your emails, mark them as read/unread, archived/unarchived, spammed/unspammed, or simply delete them.

Github

Connect your Github account to Yawmi and turn any item into a task. You can also conveniently view your open pull requests, open issues, the pull requests awaiting your review, or take advantage of the search function as if you were in Github.

Islamic Prayer Times

Enable your location to take advantage of the Islamic Prayer Times integration. This integration essentially allows you to consult your daily prayers right in Yawmi. In the settings menu, you can configure your preferred madhab, the asr prayer calculation method, and enable notifications to make sure you always pray your Salah on time.
